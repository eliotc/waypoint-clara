# Admissions at Kingsford University

## How to Apply
All applications for undergraduate study are submitted through the Victorian Tertiary Admissions Centre (VTAC). Postgraduate applications are made directly through the Kingsford University website at admissions.kingsford.edu.au.

- **Guarantee Entry rounds**: September–November 2026
- **Change of Preference period**: December 2026
- **Offers Round 1**: 14 January 2027
- **Offers Round 2**: 21 January 2027
- **Semester 1 start**: 2 March 2027

## ATAR and Entry Requirements

### Understanding ATAR
The Australian Tertiary Admission Rank (ATAR) is a percentile rank between 0.00 and 99.95. It compares your results with all other Year 12 students in Australia. Most Kingsford undergraduate degrees have an ATAR cutoff, but this is the **lowest ATAR that received an offer in the previous year** — not a minimum requirement.

### Alternative Entry Pathways
You don't need a high ATAR to study at Kingsford. Alternative entry options include:
- **TAFE/VET pathway**: Complete a relevant TAFE certificate or diploma to gain entry
- **Mature-age entry**: Applicants aged 21+ can apply based on work experience and a personal statement
- **Foundation Studies**: Kingsford's own Foundation program for students who don't meet direct entry requirements
- **Special Entry Access Scheme (SEAS)**: For students whose ATAR was affected by personal, family, or health circumstances
- **Portfolio entry**: Available for Digital Media and some Arts programs

## English Language Requirements
For international students, minimum English requirements are:
- **IELTS**: Overall 6.5 (no band below 6.0)
- **TOEFL iBT**: Overall 79 (writing 21, speaking 18)
- **PTE Academic**: Overall 58 (no communicative skill below 50)

Students who don't meet these requirements may enter via the Kingsford English Language Program (KELP), a 10–20 week intensive pathway.

## Deferral Policy
Domestic students who receive an offer may defer their place for up to 12 months. International students should contact the International Admissions team to discuss deferral eligibility. A deferral fee of $500 applies.
