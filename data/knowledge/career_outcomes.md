# Career Outcomes and Graduate Employment at Kingsford University

## Graduate Employment Overview
Kingsford's 2025 Graduate Outcomes Survey (GOC) shows:
- **88%** of Kingsford graduates are employed or in further study within 4 months of graduating
- **82%** are in full-time employment within 12 months
- Median starting salary: **AUD $72,000** (undergraduate) / **AUD $91,000** (postgraduate)
- Student-to-careers-staff ratio: 1:180 (above sector average)

## Industry Connections

### Industry Placement Programs
All undergraduate degrees include at least one industry placement or work-integrated learning (WIL) experience:
- **Engineering**: 12-week industry placement in Year 3 (partner companies include Seek, REA Group, Fujitsu)
- **Nursing/OT**: 800+ hours of clinical placement across Melbourne hospital networks
- **Business**: Consulting project with a real client organisation in Year 2
- **Digital Media**: Portfolio-building internship in Semester 2 of Year 3

### Industry Advisory Boards
Each faculty has an Industry Advisory Board that shapes curriculum and opens doors for students. Members include executives from Atlassian, Macquarie Bank, Alfred Health, and the ABC.

## The Kingsford Career Centre

### Services (all free for current students and recent graduates)
- **Career counselling**: One-on-one sessions with a career adviser. Bookable online.
- **Resume and LinkedIn reviews**: Same-week turnaround
- **Mock interviews**: Practice with industry professionals
- **Job Board**: 2,000+ active listings, filtered by faculty and study level
- **Internship Matching**: Curated matches to paid internships with 150+ partner organisations
- **Careers Fair**: April and September each year. 100+ employers on campus.
- **Graduate programs database**: Searchable list of graduate programs open to Kingsford students

### Alumni Network
Kingsford has 85,000+ alumni globally, with a strong Melbourne network. The Alumni Mentoring Program connects current students with alumni in their target industry for 6-month mentoring relationships. Apply each March and August.

## Postgraduate Study Pathways
Some careers require postgraduate study after an undergraduate degree:
- **Medicine**: Kingsford is a feeder for Graduate Medical Programs at Melbourne and Monash. Strong science GPA + GAMSAT required.
- **Law**: Kingsford does not offer law, but Psychology and Business graduates commonly combine with a Juris Doctor at another institution.
- **Psychology registration**: Requires an accredited 4th-year Honours + 2-year Masters or 3-year Doctor of Psychology.
- **Teaching (secondary)**: Bachelor graduates can complete the Master of Education (2 years) to become a qualified teacher.
- **Nursing → Nurse Practitioner**: Registered nurses with 3+ years experience can apply for the Nurse Practitioner program.

## Entrepreneurship
The **Kingsford Startup Accelerator** supports student-founded ventures with:
- Mentoring from experienced founders and investors
- $5,000 seed grants (competitive, 10 granted per year)
- Hot-desk space in The Hive (Business School)
- Access to Melbourne's startup ecosystem via the Startup Victoria network

Notable Kingsford alumni ventures include a logistics SaaS company (valued at $50M+) and a health-tech platform currently in clinical trials.
