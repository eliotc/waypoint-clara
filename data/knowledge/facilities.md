# Facilities at Kingsford University

## Campus Overview
Kingsford University's main campus spans 28 hectares in Melbourne's inner-north. Five faculty precincts surround a central green (the "Quad"), with the library, student hub, and sports complex forming the campus spine.

## Academic Facilities

### Engineering Precinct
- **Innovation Lab**: Fully equipped electronics workshop, PCB fabrication, soldering stations, and oscilloscopes. Open to all Engineering & Technology students.
- **Cybersecurity Lab (CyberLab)**: Air-gapped network environment for ethical hacking, penetration testing practicals, and security research. Requires swipe-card access (enrolled students only).
- **High-Performance Computing Cluster**: 48-node GPU cluster for AI/ML coursework and research. Bookable via the student portal in 4-hour blocks.
- **Cloud Studio**: Dedicated space for cloud architecture coursework with always-on GCP, AWS, and Azure sandbox environments.

### Health Sciences Precinct
- **Clinical Simulation Centre**: 12 fully equipped hospital-style simulation rooms, including a mock ICU and operating theatre. Used for Nursing and OT clinical skills training.
- **Anatomy Suite**: State-of-the-art anatomy teaching facility with real specimens and digital anatomy tables (Anatomage).
- **Allied Health Clinics**: Student-run physiotherapy and occupational therapy outpatient clinics, supervised by qualified clinicians (appointments open to the public).

### Business School
- **Trading Floor**: A Bloomberg-terminal-equipped trading simulation room with 30 workstations. Used for Finance coursework and investment competitions.
- **The Hive (co-working space)**: Open workspace for business students and entrepreneurs, with hot desks, meeting rooms, and a pitch space. Home to the Kingsford Startup Accelerator.

### Creative Arts Building
- **Film Studio**: Professional broadcast studio with green screen, lighting rig, and editing suites running Adobe Premiere and DaVinci Resolve.
- **UX Design Lab**: 20 workstations with Figma, Adobe XD, and eye-tracking equipment for usability research.
- **Recording Studio**: Professional-grade audio recording studio available to Digital Media students by booking.
- **Photography Dark Room**: Traditional film photography development facility (one of the last in Melbourne).

## Library

### Kingsford Central Library
- **Hours**: 24/7 during semester; 8am–8pm outside semester (student ID required after 10pm)
- **Collection**: 800,000+ items including physical books, journals, and digital resources
- **Databases**: Access to JSTOR, Scopus, IEEE Xplore, PubMed, Bloomberg, and 50+ specialist databases (accessible on- and off-campus)
- **Digital Innovation Lab (DigiLab)**: 3D printers, laser cutters, VR headsets, and a maker space. Bookable in 2-hour sessions.
- **Silent Study Zone**: Level 3 — phones off, no group work, strictly enforced
- **Collaborative Spaces**: Levels 1–2 — group study rooms (6-person, bookable 24 hours in advance via the library app)
- **Research Consultations**: Specialist librarians for Engineering, Health, Business, and Humanities. Free, bookable online.

## Sports Complex

### Facilities
- **25m Indoor Pool**: Lane swimming and aqua aerobics classes. Open 6am–9pm weekdays, 7am–6pm weekends.
- **Gymnasium**: 200+ stations including free weights, cardio machines, and a functional training area. $150/semester, free for scholarship holders and student athletes.
- **Multipurpose Courts**: Basketball, netball, and volleyball (Indoor). Bookable via the sports portal.
- **Athletics Track**: 400m synthetic track and throwing/jumping facilities. Open access.
- **Yoga Studio**: 20+ group fitness classes per week including yoga, pilates, HIIT, and spin. Included with gym membership.
- **Rock Climbing Wall**: 10m indoor wall. Equipment hire available. Beginner sessions run every Wednesday 5–7pm.

## Student Hub
The Student Hub is the social heart of campus, located in the centre of the Quad. It houses:
- **The Hub Café**: Great coffee, breakfast, and daily lunch specials ($10)
- **Post Office and ATM**
- **Student Lounge**: Bean bags, ping-pong tables, charging stations
- **Student Association offices** (student government, clubs and societies)
- **Print and Copy Centre**: Black-and-white printing 5c/page, colour 20c/page

## Campus Services

### IT Services
- **Free Wi-Fi**: eduroam across the entire campus (log in with your student credentials). Speed: up to 1Gbps in teaching buildings.
- **Student Laptops**: Lending pool of 200 laptops available for same-day borrowing from the library (4-hour or overnight loans)
- **Software**: Microsoft 365, Adobe Creative Cloud, MATLAB, and SPSS available free to enrolled students via the software portal
- **IT Help Desk**: Level 1, Library. Walk-in or online helpdesk.kingsford.edu.au.

### Sustainability
- Kingsford has been carbon neutral since 2023 (electricity from renewable sources, offset program for residual emissions)
- All campus buildings have a Green Star rating of 5 or above
- Free filtered water stations throughout campus (bring your own bottle — single-use plastic bottles not sold on campus)
- Bike repair station and air pump near the main gate

### Parking
Limited permit parking is available at $800/year (student rate) in Car Park C (south of campus). A waiting list typically applies. Park-and-ride from Glenroy Station is recommended for students arriving by car.

### Accessibility
All campus buildings are wheelchair accessible. Accessible bathrooms are located on every floor. Assistance animals are welcome on campus. For specific accessibility requirements (hearing loops, braille signage, etc.), contact access@kingsford.edu.au.
